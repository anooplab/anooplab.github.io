Research interests

Topics of current interest

  • Computational exploration of prebiotic reactions.
  • Structural evolution of atomic and nanoclusters.
  • Software development for automated identification of reaction paths and complex structures.
  • Developing automated workflows to study complex reactions.

Representative references (as listed on the original Research page):

  • A tabu-search based strategy for modelling molecular aggregates and binary reactions (Computational and Theoretical Chemistry, 2017).
  • Prebiotic chemistry of HCN tetramerisation by automated reaction search (Chemistry—A European Journal, 2018).
  • Exploring the chemical space of C2H3NO isomers and bimolecular reactions (ACS Earth and Space Chemistry, 2023).
  • Potential prebiotic pathways in extraterrestrial atmosphere: HCN and NH3 reactions (ACS Earth and Space Chemistry, 2024).
  • A global optimiser for nanoclusters (Frontiers in Chemistry, 2019).
